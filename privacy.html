Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

DailyDoseMovies.store ("we", "us") provides free creator tools hosted on GitHub Pages. This policy explains how we handle data and what information may be collected.

No sign-ups / minimum data

We do not ask users to register, log in, or submit personal data to use the core tools. All generators run in your browser — no accounts are required.

Third-party services

  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve and measure ads. Your interactions with ads are handled by Google according to their policies.
  • Hosting: The website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub). GitHub may collect access logs as described in their privacy policy.

Cookies & local storage

We do not set analytics cookies ourselves. Third-party ad providers may set cookies. Your browser settings control cookie behavior; you can block or clear cookies at any time.

Data retention

Because the site does not collect accounts or form submissions (unless you contact us voluntarily via email), we do not retain personal data. If you email us, we may keep your message to reply.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS. While we take reasonable steps to protect data, no internet service is fully secure — avoid sharing sensitive personal information via public forms or emails.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Use the Contact page.
